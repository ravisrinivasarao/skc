About Us



Dr. N. SHAILAJA
MBBS, MS(OBG), DGO, DNB
MRCOG (UK), FICOG, FCLS, PGDMLE

Dr. Shailaja considers her ability to care for women both her calling and an enormous blessing. She wants to ensure that women have access to the most advanced and up to date services. This affords competent care and wider range of treatment options.

With a desire to excel she pursued her education and completed her DGO from Kempegowda Institute of Medical Sciences, Bangalore. She is a gold medalist in OBG from Rajiv Gandhi University. She completed her MS training from JNMC (Belgaum).

She has undergone basic and higher specialist training in UroGynaecology, Laproscopic Surgery, USG and all aspects of Obstetrics & Gynaecology in India and abroad. During her training in she has worked with experts in the field of minimal invasive gynaecologic surgery, Gynae oncology, Maternal Medicine and uro-gynaecology. She completed her MRCOG (Final) in 2015. She is a Member of Royal College of Obstetrics & Gynaecology,London.

Dr. Shailaja has keen interest in High Risk Pregnancy care, Urogynaecology , endoscopy and provides her services in women's healthcare.

She is also a dedicated teacher and tries to instill her vigor and optimism to her students and encourages them to bloom to their best. Her zeal to learn and excel is what makes her stand out from the rest. She is interested in research activities has many publications, and articles to her credit. She is a member of many prestigious societies like BSOG, FOGSI, Gynaec Oncologic society, Indain association of Gynaec Endoscopy, PCOS society, DIPSI.

She is ever willing to take up responsibilities and believes in discharging her duties with utmost dedication and care.
